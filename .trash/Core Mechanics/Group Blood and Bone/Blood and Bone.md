Your character’s survival depends on managing their Blood—their life force—as well as the Injuries they sustain in battle. Blood reflects the vital energy flowing through the body, and once it is depleted, the body begins to shut down. It includes:  
- **Blood**, representing the core life force that keeps your character alive.  
- **Injuries**, representing the lasting harm and trauma that can affect performance and impose Conditions.  
- Rest and Recovery, representing how characters recover Sturdiness, Blood and other resources over time.
- **Death**, representing the consequences when Blood is fully depleted and the character is at risk of permanent loss.
