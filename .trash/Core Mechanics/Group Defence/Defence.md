Defence covers your character’s ability to avoid or absorb damage. It includes:
- **Evasion**, representing agility, reflexes, and awareness to dodge attacks before they hit.
- **Sturdiness**, representing physical resilience and training to absorb and withstand damage once an attack lands.
- **Defensive Stances**, representing a character's opinions to actively defend themselves.
- **Armour** and **Shields**, representing equipment designed to mitigate damage and enhance defensive capabilities.
