Your character’s stability depends on maintaining their **Sanity** — the clarity and self-control that holds the mind together under strain. The Hollowdeep does not only threaten the body; it wears down thought, memory, identity, and will. Mental endurance shapes how your character responds to fear, stress, trauma, magic, and the unknown. It includes:
- **Sanity**, representing the inner balance and mental resilience of your character.
- **Afflictions**, representing temporary or lasting disturbances to the mind that impose penalties or influence behaviour.
- **Rest and Recovery**, representing how characters restore mental steadiness over time through rest, reflection, grounding, or treatment.
