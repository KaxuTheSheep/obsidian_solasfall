Origins represent your character’s past, upbringing, and formative experiences. You may **create your own Origin**, subject to GM approval to ensure it fits the world. Each Origin grants [[skills]], currency, and a **starting ability** that reflects your background.

### Origin Creation
- **Name:** Give your Origin a title that fits your background and story.
- **[[Skills]]:** Gain training in **2 [[skills]]** relevant to your Origin.
- **Starting Currency:** 10d10 [[Gilded Mints]].
### Starting Abilities
Pick **one** ability that reflects your Origin:
###### **Silver Tongue**
Once per [[Rest & Recovery|rest]], reroll a failed Negotation Test.

###### **Streetwise**
Advantage on checks to navigate urban environments or interact with underworld contacts.

###### **[[Healer]]’s Touch**
When stabilising a creature, restore **1d4 [[Blood]]**.

###### **Battle Hardened**
Reduce [[Damage Types|damage]] from the first hit you take in combat by **2**.

###### **Sharp Eye**
Gain +1 training in the **Perception** [[Skills|skill]].

###### **Resourceful**
Once per day, produce a [[common]] tool or piece of gear you “happen to have.”

###### **Lucky Break**
Once per long [[Rest & Recovery|rest]], turn a failed roll into a success.

###### **Quick Reflexes**
Once per combat, when an enemy declares an [[Attacks|Attack]] or effect that includes you as a target (before any [[Attacks|Attack]] roll or effect resolution), you may immediately move up to **half your Walking [[Movement Types|Distance]]** at [[Speed & Resolution|Speed]] 14. You count as targeted at the moment the enemy declares intent, even if the effect also targets other creatures or areas.

###### **Craftsman**
You can repair broken gear **twice as fast** and create simple items given time and materials.

###### **Lore Keeper**
Gain +1 training in **Arcana** and **History** [[skills]].

###### **Intimidating Presence**
Once per [[Rest & Recovery|rest]], force an enemy to make a DN 16 Resolve Check or be Frightened for 1d4 [[Rounds]].

###### **Survivor’s Instinct**
Once per [[Rest & Recovery|Rest]], automatically detect the presence of hostile creatures within a short [[Movement Types|distance]], even if they are hidden.
