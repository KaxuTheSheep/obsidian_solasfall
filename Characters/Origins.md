Origins represent your character’s past, upbringing, and formative experiences. You may **create your own Origin**, subject to GM approval to ensure it fits the world. Each Origin grants skills, currency, and a **starting ability** that reflects your background.

### Origin Creation
- **Name:** Give your Origin a title that fits your background and story.
- **Skills:** Gain training in **2 skills** relevant to your Origin.
- **Starting Currency:** 10d10 Gilded Mints.
### Starting Abilities
Pick **one** ability that reflects your Origin:
###### **Silver Tongue**
Once per rest, reroll a failed Persuasion check.

###### **Streetwise**
Advantage on checks to navigate urban environments or interact with underworld contacts.

###### **Healer’s Touch**
When stabilising a creature, restore **1d4 Blood**.

###### **Battle Hardened**
Reduce damage from the first hit you take in combat by **2**.

###### **Sharp Eye**
Gain +1 training in the **Perception** skill.

###### **Resourceful**
Once per day, produce a common tool or piece of gear you “happen to have.”

###### **Lucky Break**
Once per long rest, turn a failed roll into a success.

###### **Quick Reflexes**
Once per combat, when an enemy declares an Attack or effect that includes you as a target (before any Attack roll or effect resolution), you may immediately move up to **half your Walking Distance** at Speed 14. You count as targeted at the moment the enemy declares intent, even if the effect also targets other creatures or areas.

###### **Craftsman**
You can repair broken gear **twice as fast** and create simple items given time and materials.

###### **Lore Keeper**
Gain +1 training in **Arcana** and **History** skills.

###### **Intimidating Presence**
Once per rest, force an enemy to make a DN 16 Resolve Check or be Frightened for 1d4 RoundRound.

###### **Survivor’s Instinct**
Once per Rest, automatically detect the presence of hostile creatures within a short distance, even if they are hidden.
