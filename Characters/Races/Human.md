## Status
*Dominant, privileged, adaptable.*

Humans control the Nobility, most government positions, and the Royal Mage academies. They are seen as the most teachable and capable of mastering magic, reinforcing their institutional dominance. Other races often view humans as entitled or imperialist but grudgingly acknowledge their organisational power.

## Culture
Family structures follow traditional patterns, emphasising nuclear and extended family bonds, though customs vary between communities. Worldviews are diverse and shaped by personal experience and social standing.

Religion and magic are deeply woven into daily life, with humans often leading major Sanctums and magical institutions, reinforcing their social and political dominance.

## Relationships with Other Races
Humans hold an exploitative stance toward other races due to their dominant societal position. Individuals, especially among Explorers, Rebels, and certain clergy, may form meaningful friendships and alliances with other groups.

Humans have a positive view of Kenku and Murks, fostered in part by shared religious ties. They are neutral toward Drenek but tend to distrust or look down on Velkhari, Stitched, and Cindral.

Most major factions, including the Royal Advisers, Merchant Guild, and Magic Institutions, are human-led.

## Appearance
Humans in Solasfall display diverse physical traits, with a wide variety of skin tones, hair colours, and eye colours. Clothing, hairstyle, and ornamentation vary based on social standing, occupation, and personal taste. They generally have medium builds and average height compared to other races, adaptable to a wide range of roles and environments.
