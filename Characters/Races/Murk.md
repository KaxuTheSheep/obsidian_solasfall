### Status:
Alien but accepted
- Amphibious and tall, with grey-green skin and long limbs, Murks are a quiet but present part of the city.
- Often found in the Market and [[Entertainment District]], where their flexibility and [[Speed & Resolution|speed]] are valued.
- Generally scattered throughout [[Solasfall]], but hold lower social status — slightly beneath [[Kenku]] in societal standing.
- Though alien in appearance, Murks are tolerated rather than feared or pitied.
- Valued for physical capability and craftsmanship, though often underestimated or overlooked.

### Culture:
- Murks favour learning practical, physical [[skills]] — [[Skills|crafting]], combat, and [[Skills|endurance]]-based trades.
- Their culture is shaped by a sense of impermanence; they accept their shorter lifespans and seek to live fully.
- Social bonds resemble loose communal groupings, more fluid than rigid family units, akin to amphibious spawning pools or seasonal gatherings.
- [[The Emberwright]] [[Faith]] is [[common]] among Murks, resonating with their view of life as a fleeting forge of purpose.
- They maintain a quiet, patient outlook toward hardship and change, preferring [[Actions|action]] over debate.
- Within their districts, Murks thrive as artisans, warriors, and traders — [[roles]] they may be denied elsewhere. Their neighbourhoods preserve old traditions from their surface [[origins]], maintaining ancestral rites, symbols, and seasonal ceremonies.

### Relationships with Other [[Races]]:
- **[[Human|Humans]]** — Viewed as weak-bodied but admired for their perseverance and adaptability.
- **[[Stitched]]** — Fascinating to Murks; they are intrigued by the paradox of deathless decay.
- **[[Kenku]]** — Murks tend to dislike [[Kenku]], finding them too opportunistic or performative.
- **[[Velkhari]]** — Seen as strong but lazy, obsessed with fighting rather than purpose.
- **[[Drenek]]** — Murks are confused by them, curious but often uncertain how to relate.
- **[[Cindral]]** — Distrusted deeply; Murks believe [[Cindral]] betrayed something sacred in the fire or forge.

### Appearance:
- Murks are amphibious, with smooth, moist skin ranging from green-grey to slate-blue hues.
- Their eyes are large and reflective, adapted to dim light, and their limbs are long and flexible.
- Clothing tends toward fitted wraps and waterproof fabrics, designed for [[Movement Types|movement]] both on land and underwater.
- Many decorate their skin with bioluminescent inks or carved symbols that glow faintly in the dark.

### Traits:
##### Amphibious Breathing:
You can breathe underwater indefinitely and on land.
##### Water Adaptation:
You have advantage on [[Skills|Athletics]] checks related to swimming and climbing slippery or wet surfaces.
##### Low Light [[Facing & Vision|Vision]]:
You can see in dim light as if it were bright light for 9 meters. You cannot see in total darkness.
##### Swift Swimmer:
Whenever you make an [[Attacks|attack]] while underwater you are able to move a number of meters equal to half your swimming [[Movement Types|distance]].
##### Tongue Lash:
As an [[Attacks|Attack]]-type [[Actions|action]], you may lash out with your tongue as a Half [[Actions|Action]] at a target within 4.5 meters. This uses your **Grace** [[Attributes|attribute]] for [[Attacks|Attack]] rolls and deals 1d4 Bludgeoning [[Damage Types|Damage]] on a hit ([[Speed & Resolution|Speed]] 14). 
You can also use your tongue to grab or retrieve unattended small objects within [[Range & Threat|range]], though not to manipulate tools or [[weapons]].
##### Enduring Craftsmanship:
Gain +1 training in one of the following [[Skills]]: **[[Skills|Crafting]]**, **[[Skills|Endurance]]**, or **[[Skills|Survival]]**.
