### Status:
Pitied, feared, and often treated as expendable
- Stitched lack traditional family structures but form strong bonds primarily with [[the Pale Bloom]] Sanctum and The Explorers' Guild.
- Must “rethread” themselves during Long Rests to maintain cohesion; failure leads to physical disintegration and loss of identity.
- Younger Stitched are aloof and confused, flooded by conflicting past-life glimpses; older ones forge stable identities over time.
- Seen as either blessed or cursed by undeath; opinions vary even among themselves.
- Widely used as tireless labourers or explorers due to their resilience and lack of physical needs.
- Feared by the public due to prior use as [[The Hollow Shade|Hollow Shade]] Cultist's cannon fodder during wars with [[The Knights Orders]].
- Despised by [[The Verdant Hand]] for their unnatural stasis and resistance to natural cycles of growth and decay.

### Culture:
- Identity is fluid in early stages and gradually crystallizes around one or more dominant past lives.
- Emotional expression is muted; most avoid forming more than a handful of close bonds (often no more than 3).
- It is taboo to flaunt recognition or connection with former loved ones; jealousy and grief run deep.
- A Stitched’s personality often mirrors whichever former life aligns with their current function (e.g., explorers skew toward warrior-like behaviour).

### Relationships with Other [[Races]]:
- Most [[races]] regard them with unease or fear, associating them with war trauma and necromantic taboo.
- They are accepted primarily for their usefulness and quiet endurance.
- [[Human|Humans]] and [[Cindral]] tend to view them with mistrust, while [[the Pale Bloom]] Sanctum shows them cautious reverence.
- [[Velkhari]] respect their endurance but do not form lasting bonds.

### Appearance:
- Stitched are clearly artificial, with visible seams, patches, and varying skin tones from their previous lives.
- Body parts may differ in size, color, and texture; older Stitched tend to appear more cohesive and symmetrical.
- Eyes often glow faintly, reflecting the residual vitality of past lives.
- Clothing is practical, adapted for exploration, labor, or stealth, often covering or reinforcing seams.

### Traits:
##### Patchwork Form:
Your stitched-together body can separate into autonomous parts.
    - As a half [[Actions|action]], you may detach one body part (hand, arm, leg, head, eye, etc.) at [[Speed & Resolution|Speed]] 13.
    - Detached parts have 4.5 m of [[Movement Types|movement]] [[Movement Types|distance]].
    - Detached parts share your [[Blood]] and [[Sturdiness]] pools and cannot act independently — all [[actions]], [[Movement Types|movement]], and abilities come from your own turn’s resources.
    - Detached parts retain relevant senses (a head can see and hear; a hand can feel; a removed eye can see, etc.). Speaking requires lungs.
    - Detached hands can perform the somatic components of spells if directed.
    - As a half [[Actions|action]], you can attach detached limbs at [[Speed & Resolution|Speed]] 12.
    - You can replace missing body parts with those from another creature. Doing so requires a Long [[Rest & Recovery|Rest]] to attune to the new part.
##### Unravelling: 
Once per Long [[Rest & Recovery|Rest]], when you would be reduced to 0 [[Blood]], you instead drop to 1 [[Blood]].
