### Status:
Feared, tolerated, and respected for strength
- Created by a parasitic entity from the [[Hollowdeep]] that transforms [[Human|humans]] into something new.
- Marked by pale skin, shark-like teeth, pupil-less eyes, and long tongues often mistaken for demons.
- Tolerated only for their martial usefulness and ability to resonate with [[Rift]] energy.
- Most faithful distrust them, though they're not barred from participating in [[religion]].
- Their transformations are taboo within city walls, even when offered as a form of "rebirth" to the old or dying.

### Culture:
- Velkhari form no more than three meaningful bonds; casual associations are [[common]], but trust is rare.
- The transformation overwrites the old self entirely; no memories or feelings of their past life remain.
- In their own district, they thrive in a competitive sparring culture, maintaining a loose but respected hierarchy based on [[Skills|skill]] and combat prowess.
- Natural-born Velkhari are rare and are trained in basic [[Skills|survival]] and combat [[roles]] upon arrival.
- Their perspective on the [[Hollowdeep]] varies: some see it as home, others as a place of growth or challenge.

### Relationships with Other [[Races]]:
- Widely mistrusted, especially by [[Human|Humans]] and the faithful.
- Generally tolerated due to their usefulness and fearsome reputation; people avoid provoking them.
- [[Drenek]] and [[Murk|Murks]] may respect their power but keep a wide emotional [[Movement Types|distance]].
- They treat others case-by-case, forming opinions based on individual conduct rather than [[Races|race]].

### Appearance:
- Pale, almost ghostly skin with shark-like teeth and long tongues.
- Pupil-less eyes that reflect [[Rift]] energy.
- Typically wear minimal, practical clothing suited for combat or climbing.
- Their bodies often show faint [[Rift]] markings or scars from transformation rituals.

### Traits:
##### [[Rift]] Step:
Harnessing their connection to the [[Rift]], Velkhari can momentarily tear open a shimmering portal in space.
- As a Half [[Actions|Action]], create a [[Rift]] within your line of sight and instantly teleport to it, up to 15 m away ([[Speed & Resolution|Speed]] 10).
- Can be used once per [[Rest & Recovery|Rest]].
##### Spider Climb:
- You can crawl on vertical walls and ceilings.
##### Martial Prowess:
- Gain +1 to all melee [[Attacks|Attack]] rolls.
##### Intimidating Presence:
- Gain +1 training in [[Skills|Intimidation]] [[Skills|skill]].
