### Overview
Diplomat is the silver tongue of the party, excelling in swaying opinions, forging alliances, and manipulating social situations to the group’s advantage. People may refer to Diplomats with curiosity or suspicion, knowing they have a way of turning words into [[Actions|action]].

### [[Roles|Role]] Benefits
- Social Savant: Gain +1 training in Negotiation.
- Inspiring Words: Once per [[Rest & Recovery|rest]], you can grant one ally a bonus equal to your Presence on any roll within the next minute.
- Social Influence: You gain advantage on any social checks when acting as a representative or spokesperson for your group.
- Subtle Manipulation: Once per combat, you can force an enemy to reroll a successful [[Attacks|attack]] or [[Skills|skill]] check against you or an ally.
