### Overview
Healers are the lifeblood of any expedition, mending both flesh and spirit when Hollowdeep’s dangers cut too deep. They stand at the edge of despair, turning pain into purpose and keeping their allies alive through skill, intuition, and unwavering calm. Whether trained in medicine, divine rites, or alchemical restoration, a Healer’s presence is often the difference between triumph and tragedy.

### Role Benefits
- **Insightful Care:** Gain +1 training in Medicine.
- **Efficient Healing:** When using healing potions or magic, increase Blood restored by 1 Blood Die.
- **Soothing Resolve:** Once per combat, you can remove one level of Exhaustion from a creature that can see or hear you as a free Action.
