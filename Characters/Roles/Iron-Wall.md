### Overview
Those who take up the mantle of the Iron-Wall are more than armoured guardians — they are the line that does not break. Whether a knight sworn to noble vows or a hardened sellsword with nothing left to lose, the Iron-Wall’s purpose is to endure where others falter. In Solasfall’s perilous ventures and Hollowdeep’s descent, their steadiness becomes the anchor that keeps companions alive. Many explorers say that when an Iron-Wall stands their ground, even the dark seems to hesitate.

### Role Benefits
- **Sturdiness Focus:** At the start of each combat, gain temporary _Sturdiness_ equal to half your level (rounded up). This temporary Sturdiness is added to your current pool and is lost before normal Sturdiness. It vanishes when combat ends.
- **Damage Absorption:** When you use the _Defend_ action, the damage reduction increases by +1 die roll (in addition to your weapon dice).
- **Taunt:** Once per combat, you can force one enemy within melee range to target you with their next attack.
