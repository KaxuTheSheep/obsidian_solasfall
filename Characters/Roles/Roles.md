_Purpose given form, and function shaped by necessity._

In the shifting depths of Hollowdeep and the perilous streets of Solasfall, no one survives alone. Adventurers, mercenaries, and delvers alike adopt _Roles_ — loose archetypes born from years of expeditionary tradition and battlefield pragmatism. Though not official ranks, these labels have become common shorthand among The Explorers' Guild, mercenary bands, and even the city’s tavern talk.

A Role defines how one contributes to the group’s survival — not through grand destiny, but through instinct, training, and grit. They grant small but meaningful benefits in combat and exploration, reflecting the hard-earned habits of those who’ve endured countless trials.

While Roles hold little weight in Royal Courts or religious orders, those who understand the rhythm of delving — the explorers, scouts, and Hollowdeep veterans — speak of them with respect. They’re not titles, but acknowledgements of purpose.

**Recognised Roles of Solasfall:**

- Iron-Wall
- Vanguard
- Striker
- Healer
- Wayfinder
- Tactician
- Diplomat
- Scholar
