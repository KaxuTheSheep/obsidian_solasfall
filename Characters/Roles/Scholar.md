### Overview
Scholar delves into knowledge, lore, and arcane mysteries, providing the party with insight and minor magical utility. Scholars are often quietly respected by academics and adventurers alike, though some dismiss them as overly cautious or bookish.

### Role Benefits
- Lore Master: Gain +1 training in Arcana and History.
- Quick Study: Once per rest, you may reroll a failed Investigation or Arcana check.
- Arcane Utility: Gain a simple magical cantrip (to be chosen or developed) usable once per combat or twice per rest.
- Insightful Analysis: Allies within 10 feet gain +1 on all rolls related to lore, magic, or puzzles.
