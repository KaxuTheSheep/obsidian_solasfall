### Overview
Strikers are the unseen blades and silent shots that decide battles before they begin. They specialise in precision — hitting weak points, exploiting distractions, and striking from vantage or shadow. Whether wielding a bow, firearm, or thrown blade, a Striker’s focus turns [[Movement Types|distance]] into dominance. In [[Solasfall]], they are often hunters, assassins, or scouts — the quiet professionals whose impact is measured in the silence that follows.

### [[Roles|Role]] Benefits
- **Ranged Bonus:** Gain +1 to all ranged [[Attacks|attack]] rolls.
- **Precision Shot:** Once per combat, you can add +5 to the [[Damage Types|damage]] of a ranged [[Attacks|attack]].
- **Weak Spot:** When you hit an enemy with a ranged [[Attacks|attack]], you reduce their [[Evasion]] by 1 until the start of your next turn.
