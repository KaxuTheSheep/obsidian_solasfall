### Overview
Strikers are the unseen blades and silent shots that decide battles before they begin. They specialise in precision — hitting weak points, exploiting distractions, and striking from vantage or shadow. Whether wielding a bow, firearm, or thrown blade, a Striker’s focus turns distance into dominance. In Solasfall, they are often hunters, assassins, or scouts — the quiet professionals whose impact is measured in the silence that follows.

### Role Benefits
- **Ranged Bonus:** Gain +1 to all ranged attack rolls.
- **Precision Shot:** Once per combat, you can add +5 to the damage of a ranged attack.
- **Weak Spot:** When you hit an enemy with a ranged attack, you reduce their Evasion by 1 until the start of your next turn.
