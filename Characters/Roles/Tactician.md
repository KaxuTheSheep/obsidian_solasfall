### Overview
Tacticians are the masters of battlefield control, shaping the flow of combat with precision, foresight, and strategic command. They control flow of engagements, coordinate allies, and disrupt enemies with clever maneuvers. Their presence can turn chaotic skirmishes into well-orchestrated victories, and their influence is recognized both by comrades and cautious opponents.

### [[Roles|Role]] Benefits
- **Battlefield Control:** Gain +1 training in [[Skills|Intimidation]].
- **Debilitating Orders:** Once per combat, you can impose a Slowed or Disoriented [[Conditions|condition]] on up to two enemies within sight for 1 [[Rounds|round]]. DN 14 Presence check to activate.
- **Tactical [[Movement Types|Movement]]:** Allies within 10 feet of you gain +2 to their [[Evasion]] for their next turn.
- **Commanding Presence:** Once per combat, you can grant an ally advantage on their next [[Attacks|attack]] or [[Skills|skill]] check.
