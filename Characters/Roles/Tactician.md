### Overview
Tacticians are the masters of battlefield control, shaping the flow of combat with precision, foresight, and strategic command. They control flow of engagements, coordinate allies, and disrupt enemies with clever maneuvers. Their presence can turn chaotic skirmishes into well-orchestrated victories, and their influence is recognized both by comrades and cautious opponents.

### Role Benefits
- **Battlefield Control:** Gain +1 training in Intimidation.
- **Debilitating Orders:** Once per combat, you can impose a Slowed or Disoriented condition on up to two enemies within sight for 1 round. DN 14 Presence check to activate.
- **Tactical Movement:** Allies within 10 feet of you gain +2 to their Evasion for their next turn.
- **Commanding Presence:** Once per combat, you can grant an ally advantage on their next attack or skill check.
