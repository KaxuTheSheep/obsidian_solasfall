### Overview
Vanguards are the first into battle and often the last to retreat. They thrive in chaos, turning momentum into weaponry and aggression into control. Where others [[brace]] for impact, the Vanguard charges — their fury a signal to allies that the fight has begun. In the eyes of [[Solasfall]]’s citizens, Vanguards embody reckless courage, but to those who’ve fought beside them, they are the heartbeat of every assault.

### [[Roles|Role]] Benefits
- **Melee Specialist:** Gain +1 to all melee [[Attacks|attack]] rolls.
- **Brutal Strike:** Once per combat, you can roll an extra weapon [[Damage Types|damage]] die on an [[Attacks|attack]].
- **Momentum:** When you move at least 3 metres immediately before making a melee [[Attacks|attack]], the target takes 5 [[Damage Types|damage]] directly to their [[Blood]] pool in addition to normal [[Damage Types|damage]].
