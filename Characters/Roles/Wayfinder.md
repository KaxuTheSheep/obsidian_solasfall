### Overview
Wayfinders are masters of navigation and [[Skills|survival]], guiding their companions through the twisting hazards of [[Hollowdeep]] and the labyrinthine streets of [[Solasfall]] alike. Their keen senses and arcane insight allow them to anticipate danger, map unknown territory, and keep their group moving forward where others would falter. Often quiet and observant, Wayfinders are indispensable for both urban expeditions and wilderness exploration.

### [[Roles|Role]] Benefits
- **Pathfinder Instinct:** Gain +1 training in [[Skills|Survival]].
- **Arcane Mapper:** If you do not have inherent [[magic]], you start with the Pathfinder’s Watch, a magical item embedded into your nervous system. It allows you to use Pathfinding [[Magic]] by rolling a [[Skills|Perception]] Check to determine the correct direction to travel. Each use reduces the roll by -1, which refreshes on any [[Rest & Recovery|Rest]].
- **Scout’s Agility:** Increase your [[Evasion]] by 1.
