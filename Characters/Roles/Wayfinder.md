### Overview
Wayfinders are masters of navigation and survival, guiding their companions through the twisting hazards of Hollowdeep and the labyrinthine streets of Solasfall alike. Their keen senses and arcane insight allow them to anticipate danger, map unknown territory, and keep their group moving forward where others would falter. Often quiet and observant, Wayfinders are indispensable for both urban expeditions and wilderness exploration.

### Role Benefits
- **Pathfinder Instinct:** Gain +1 training in Survival.
- **Arcane Mapper:** If you do not have inherent magic, you start with the Pathfinder’s Watch, a magical item embedded into your nervous system. It allows you to use Pathfinding Magic by rolling a Perception Check to determine the correct direction to travel. Each use reduces the roll by -1, which refreshes on any Rest.
- **Scout’s Agility:** Increase your Evasion by 1.
