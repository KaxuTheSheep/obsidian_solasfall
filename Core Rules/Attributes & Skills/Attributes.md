 Your character’s **Attributes** are the core lenses through which you experience and shape Solasfall. These six qualities represent your essence—your body, mind, spirit, and influence within this fractured city. They aren’t just numbers; they tell the story of how you survive, adapt, and change the world around you.

Each Attribute is rated from 0 to 9. The higher your score, the stronger your connection to that aspect of your being.
## The Six Attributes

#### Might
The raw force of your body and will. Might isn’t just about brute strength—it’s your stubbornness to push through pain, your grip on reality when everything is falling apart. Whether breaking a lock, holding the line in combat, or enduring the gruelling weight of the Hollowdeep, Might is your anchor to physical power and endurance.

#### Grace  
The fluid art of movement and precision. Grace is how you dance through danger, slip unseen into forbidden places, or thread a needle with surgical skill. It governs not only your agility but your finesse—how you wield a blade, pick a pocket, or slip past a watcher without a sound.

#### Vitality 
The stubborn spark that keeps you alive when the world tries to snuff you out. Vitality measures your resilience—against poison, disease, exhaustion, and the gnawing corruption of the Fallows. It’s your inner fire, how long you can fight on, how deeply you breathe through the pain, and how quickly you recover.

#### Insight 
The gift to see beyond the obvious and grasp the hidden currents beneath Solasfall’s surface. Insight is your ability to read people’s truths, decipher cryptic signs, and navigate the tangled webs of politics, secrets, and lies. It’s not just intelligence—it’s your intuition, your gut feeling, your inner compass in a city drowning in shadows.

#### Resolve
The unyielding strength of spirit and purpose. Resolve fuels your conviction when the weight of despair tries to break you. It is your clarity amid madness, your quiet stubbornness to hold fast to what matters. Whether facing the whispers of Forbidden Deities or rallying others to your cause, Resolve is your anchor in a fractured world.

#### Presence 
The power to command attention, sway hearts, and bend wills. Presence is your charisma, yes—but it’s more than charm. It’s the aura that fills a room, the voice that carries over murmurs, the way you become a symbol for others to rally behind or fear. It’s the subtle magic of influence, Negotiation, and intimidation.

## Scores
Your character’s core abilities are represented by six Attributes. Each Attribute is a number from **0 to 9**, reflecting your natural talent or training in that area. Higher numbers mean you’re better at that attribute’s domain.

|**Score**|**What It Means**|
|---|---|
|0-2|Unskilled or novice|
|3-5|Competent|
|6-8|Skilled|
|9|Exceptional / expert|

## Attribute Modifiers 
Your Attribute modifier is calculated by dividing your Attribute score by 3 and rounding down. This modifier is what you add to most rolls involving that Attribute.

| **Attribute Score** | **Modifier (Attribute ÷ 3)** |
| ------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| 0-2                 | 0                            |
| 3-5                 | +1                           |
| 6-8                 | +2                           |
| 9                   | +3                           |
### Determining Your Attributes

You have **24 points** to spend on your six attributes:

**Might, Grace, Vitality, Insight, Resolve, and Presence.**

**Starting from Zero**

All your attributes start at **0**. You then spend points to increase them.

**Increasing Attributes Costs Points**

As attributes get higher, they cost more points to improve:

|**Attribute Value**|**Cost to Increase to Next Level**|
|---|---|
|0 → 1|1 point|
|1 → 2|1 point|
|2 → 3|1 point|
|3 → 4|2 points|
|4 → 5|2 points|
|5 → 6|2 points|
|6 → 7|3 points|
|7 → 8|3 points|
|8 → 9|4 points|
