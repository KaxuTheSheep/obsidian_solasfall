### Off-Hand Strikes
If you are wielding a **Light** weapon in your off-hand:
- When you make a **melee Attack** with your main-hand weapon, you may make **one Off-Hand Strike** as part of the **same Full Action**.
- The **Off-Hand Strike** uses **only the weapon’s Damage Die** (no Attribute modifier).
- The **Off-Hand Strike resolves at**:
	**Final Speed = Off-Hand Weapon Base Speed + Attribute Modifier – 2**
This represents the slight inefficiency of coordinating two weapons **without making it weak**.
#### Defensive Benefit
If you are wielding **two weapons** and declare **Parry**, you gain:
- **+1 to Parry Tests**
_(This stacks with Guarded and other modifiers.)_
#### Restrictions
- The off-hand weapon must be **Light**.
- Only **one** Off-Hand Strike per **Round**, regardless of how many Attacks you take.
