Even the strongest adventurers cannot fight endlessly. Between battles and rift incursions, rest allows the body and mind to recover from exhaustion, mend wounds, and regain lost vitality. In a city where danger never truly sleeps, opportunities for genuine rest are precious—and often determine who survives the next rift.

Rest comes in three forms, each offering different degrees of recovery depending on time, safety, and circumstance.
### Break (≈ 30 minutes)
A brief recovery during travel or downtime.
During a Break:
- Restore **Sturdiness** to **full**.
- You may spend **1 Blood Die** to recover **Blood**.
- You may spend **1 Sanity Die** to recover **Sanity**.
- Reduce **Exhaustion** by **1**.
- Perform **1 Rest Activity**.
- **Minor Wounds do _not_ heal**, but may be **treated**.
- **Attempt to reduce a Minor Affliction:** _Resolve Test (DN 16)_ → success reduces tier by 1.
> Breaks restore **combat readiness**, not **health**.

### Short Rest (≈ 2 hours)
Time to clean wounds, breathe, and recover enough to push forward.
During a Short Rest:
- Restore **Sturdiness** to **full**.
- You may spend **any number** of Blood Dice to recover **Blood**.
- You may spend **any number** of Sanity Dice to recover **Sanity**.
- Attempt to **Close Open Wounds:** _Vitality Test (DN 14)_ → On success, the wound becomes _Closed_
- Perform **up to 4 Rest Activities**.
- Reduce **Exhaustion** by **1d4**.
> Short Rests **stabilise** wounds — they **do not remove** them.

### Long Rest (≈ 2 weeks of safety)
Rare. Requires security, shelter, and absence of life-threatening stress.
During a Long Rest:
- Fully restore **Blood**, **Sturdiness**, and **Sanity**.
- Remove **all Open and Closed Wounds**.
- Reset **Exhaustion** to **0**.
- Regain all **Blood Dice** and **Sanity Dice**.
- Attempt to reduce **any Affliction** (Minor or Moderate): _Resolve Test (DN 12)_ → On success reduce tier by 1.
> **Permanent Injuries remain** until treated by medical reconstruction, divine therapy, or ritual.

### Rest Activities (≈ 30 minutes each)
Pick during Breaks and Short Rests.

| Activity           | Effect                                                               |
| ------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Catch Breath       | Spend 1 Blood Die to restore Blood.                                  |
| Patch Wounds       | _Medicine Test (DN 14)_: Close 1 Open Wound on success.              |
| Sharpen Focus      | Gain +1 to your **next** Attack or Skill Test before your next rest. |
| Keep Watch         | You + one ally gain Advantage on Perception during the rest.         |
| Cook / Eat         | Restore **Sturdiness** to full.                                      |
| Repair Gear        | Remove minor weapon/armour wear (no reforging).                      |
| Meditate / Reflect | Reduce **Exhaustion** by 1.                                          |
| Study / Research   | Learn information via relevant Skill Test.                           |
