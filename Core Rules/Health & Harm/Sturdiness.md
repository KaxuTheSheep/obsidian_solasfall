**Sturdiness** represents your character’s ability to absorb and withstand physical punishment before their vital health is compromised. It reflects endurance, toughness, and the natural resilience of the body.

**Calculation:**  
Sturdiness = Base Sturdiness + Vitality Modifier + Other Modifiers
- **Base Sturdiness:** A fixed value determined by your character’s class. This base increases each level by a dice roll, growing slightly faster than Blood and varying between classes. #Wip
- **Vitality Modifier:** Your Vitality attribute modifier, representing your physical endurance and robustness.
- **Other Modifiers:** Bonuses or penalties from magic, conditions, essences, or situational factors.
When your character takes damage, it is first absorbed by Sturdiness. Only after Sturdiness is depleted does damage begin to reduce your core health pool, known as Blood, multiple attack apply sequentially.

A high Sturdiness score means your character can shrug off many attacks without lasting harm, while low Sturdiness leaves you vulnerable to injuries that directly impact your vital health more quickly.
