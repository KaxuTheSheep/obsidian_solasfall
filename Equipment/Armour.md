Armour reduces incoming damage and influences physical manoeuvrability. It does not affect action Speed.
Equipping armour requires meeting its Might Requirement. If your Might is below this requirement, the armour cannot be used effectively.

## Armour Categories

|Tier|Armour|Might Requirement|DR|Evasion Modifier|Movement Penalty|Dodge|
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
|1|Clothing|0|0|0|None|Normal.|
|2|Leather|1|1|0|None|Normal.|
|3|Brigandine|2|2|–1|–1m Run, –1m Climb|Normal.|
|4|Chainmail|3|3|–2|–2m Run, –1m Walk, –2m Climb, –1m Jump|Full Grace bonus. Distance moved –1m.|
|5|Half Plate|4|4|–3|–3m Run, –1m Walk, –3m Climb, –2m Jump|Cannot Dodge without Training. If Trained: full Grace bonus, Distance moved –1m.|
|6|Full Plate|6|5|–4|–4m Run, –2m Walk, –4m Climb, –3m Jump|Cannot Dodge without Training. If Trained: Evasion bonus reduced to Grace ÷ 3 (rounded down), Distance moved –1m.|
