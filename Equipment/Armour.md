Armour reduces incoming damage (**DR**) and influences physical manoeuvrability.  
It does **not** affect action Speed.

Equipping armour requires meeting its **Might Requirement**.  
If the character’s **Might** is below this requirement, the armour **cannot be used** effectively.
# Armour Categories
Armour protects by reducing incoming damage (**DR**) and impacts how well you can avoid and manoeuvre in combat.  
Armour does **not** change action Speed.
