Equipment represents the tools, protection, and gear a character carries into combat or exploration.  
It influences how they **attack**, **defend**, **move**, and **endure** danger.
Equipment never changes a character’s **Attributes** directly — instead, it affects:
- Sturdiness (how long you endure physical harm)
- Blood loss potential (via reducing incoming damage)
- Evasion (how easily you are struck)
- Movement Types (how far you can reposition each round)
- Speed for certain Actions (e.g., casting, aiming, reacting)
## Categories of Equipment
- **Armour**  
  Protects the body by reducing incoming damage (**DR**).  
  Heavier Armour requires higher **Might** to wear effectively and reduces mobility.
- **Shields**  
  Defensive tools used with the **Block** action.  
  Shields enhance reactive defense rather than passive Evasion.
- **Weapons** 
  Weapons differ by **damage**, **Speed**, and later **Maneuver traits**.
