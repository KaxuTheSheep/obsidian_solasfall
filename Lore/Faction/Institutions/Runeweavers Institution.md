### Overview
The Runeweavers Institution specialises in the study and application of latent magic through intricate runes, sigils, and circles. Their craft transforms Mana into structured energy outputs, creating artefacts, enchantments, and magical devices that power much of Solasfall’s infrastructure. Every rune, whether carved in stone, inked on parchment, or tattooed on flesh, serves as a vessel for precise, disciplined magic.
Runeweavers are both artisans and engineers — their art demands grace of hand and clarity of mind. Many train obsessively to refine their dexterity, for the smallest flaw in a sigil can mean catastrophic failure. Their ability to directly interpret and replicate runic forms found within the Hollowdeep makes them invaluable to explorers and scholars alike.

## Role & Benefits
- **Artefact Crafting:** Collaborate with the Emberwright Sanctum to forge enchanted items and instruments of power.
- **Knightly Support:** Supply the Order of the Gilded Chain with runed Armour, Weapons, and wards.
- **Hollowdeep Research:** Decode and reproduce ancient runes and circles recovered from the Hollowdeep, converting them into functional spells.
- **City Infrastructure:** Maintain runic wards, lighting systems, and mana-conductive conduits across Solasfall.

## Structure
- **Headmaster:** Oversees all rune research, artefact production, and academic coordination with other institutions.
- **Faculty:** Experts in rune etching, magical geometry, and arcane metallurgy.
- **Students:** Chosen for a balance of grace and intellect; rigorous tests in both precision drawing and theoretical design determine advancement.

## Public Perception & Culture
The Runeweavers Institution is regarded as the most practical and methodical of the magical schools. To the populace, they are the quiet artisans who keep the city’s enchanted heart beating — their sigils empower the Veil’s mechanisms, reinforce buildings, and sustain everyday conveniences. To scholars, they are archivists of pattern and order; to nobles, indispensable craftsmen.
Their culture fuses artistry and engineering, blending the reverence of sacred craftsmanship with the discipline of bureaucratic precision. Each rune is both equation and prayer, written in the language of mana itself.

## Practices & Rituals
- **Runic Discipline:** Students maintain a daily regimen of precision drawing, muscle control, and meditation to stabilise mana flow through their bodies.
- **Artefact Communion:** Before activating a newly forged artefact, Runeweavers recite a litany known as _The Breath of Pattern_ — a ritual aligning Human intent with arcane structure.
- **Runic Tattoos:** Considered the highest expression of personal mastery; each design is unique to its bearer and potentially fatal if inscribed incorrectly.

## Headmaster
The Runeweavers Institution is currently led by **Archmagus Oren Valeclay**, a master of runic and latent magic. Oren oversees all aspects of artefact creation, runic research, and Hollowdeep integration, ensuring that students maintain precision, discipline, and clarity in their craft.

He maintains close professional relationships with other institutions, particularly for joint ventures in healing artefacts, expedition Equipment, and rift stabilisation. His decades of pre-Veil experience as a Drenek Scholar have made him both cautious and methodical, but his expertise commands respect across all of Solasfall’s magical community. Oren’s leadership combines the meticulous rigor of engineering with the artistry of arcane craftsmanship, making the institution a cornerstone of both city infrastructure and Hollowdeep exploration.
