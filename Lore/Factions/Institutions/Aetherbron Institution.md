### Overview
The **Aetherbron Institution** is the cornerstone of magical understanding in Solasfall, devoted to the study of **mana in its purest, unshaped form**. Its scholars believe that mastery of raw magic is the truest expression of control — the foundation from which all other schools derive.

### Philosophy & Purpose
Aetherbron teaches that Mana is the breath of existence, a universal current connecting all living and unseen things. Rather than altering or channelling it through elements or sigils, Aetherbron mages focus on direct manipulation — bending its flow, reinforcing barriers, or forging paths through unstable realms.

This Institution serves as both **guardian and guide** to Solasfall: its mages sustain the outer Veil’s protective wards and assist explorers in traversing the Hollowdeep. Through this work, Aetherbron maintains a delicate equilibrium between the mortal world and the wild, living magic beneath it.

### Magic & Study
Students of Aetherbron learn to:
- **Shape raw mana** into pure force or protective constructs.
- **Sense magical flows**, identifying rifts or distortions before they rupture.
- **Forge pathways** through Hollowdeep’s shifting terrain — a discipline called _Pathfinding_.
- **Reinforce or restore barriers** surrounding Solasfall’s Veil.
Graduates often serve as **Wayfinder**, guiding expeditions, or join Royal Mages in maintaining the Veil’s outer structure. Their versatility makes them vital to both exploration and defence.

### Role in Solasfall
Aetherbron-trained mages are deeply respected but rarely understood. While not the most flamboyant practitioners, their work underpins nearly every magical structure in the city — from protective domes to the navigation of dimensional corridors. The Institution also coordinates closely with the **Royal Mages**, supplying both expertise and personnel to sustain the Veil’s delicate network.
The Institution’s halls are quiet places of stillness, humming faintly with mana resonances used in training exercises. Many of Solasfall’s most disciplined spellcasters trace their roots back to these serene chambers.

### Headmaster
The Aetherbron Institution is currently led by **Archmagus Tyren Holl**, a reclusive master of raw and pathfinding magic. Tyren’s unmatched attunement to Mana ensures the institution remains grounded and effective, guiding both faculty and students in research, Veil maintenance, and Hollowdeep expeditions. His presence maintains unity among the staff and wards off rivalries with other Magic Institutions.
