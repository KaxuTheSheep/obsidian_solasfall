The Hollowdeep is a vast, multidimensional network of realms — not stacked vertically, but webbed, with branches leading ever deeper into the unknown. Explorers enter through a **Rift** created and maintained by the Royal Mages of Solasfall. Each floor is a self-contained world, connected by stable portals.

### Access and Time Mechanics
- **Rift Cycle:** Every 4 weeks, the Royal Mages weaken the Veil, opening a Rift to the Hollowdeep for 1 hour. All explorers enter at Floor One. The later an explorer enters during this window, the farther from the central hub they are placed, closer to the exit portals that lead to subsequent floors.
- **Shared Time Window:** Time flows equally between Solasfall and Floor One while the Rift is open.
- **Spatial Hazard Gradient:** As explorers move outward from the centre of Floor One, they face increasingly dangerous monsters, environmental hazards, and unstable terrain. Those who enter later are forced into these riskier zones immediately.
- **Temporal Shift:** Once the Rift closes, Hollowdeep time decouples — 7 Hollowdeep days pass while only 1 day passes in Solasfall.
- **Return Window:** After exactly 7 Hollowdeep-days, a portal reopens for 7 hours in hollowdeep #Wip. Failure to return results in being stranded for 28 Hollowdeep-weeks.

### Structure and Navigation
- **The Shattered Expanse:** The only floor directly linked to Solasfall; staging ground for exploration.
- **Portals:** The Shattered Expanse contains portals to four distinct Floors, leading into the branching network.
- **Nonlinear Web:** Subsequent floors have multiple exit portals. Some require Mana, rituals, or solving arcane mechanisms to activate.

### Environment and Threats
- Each floor is ecologically and thematically unique: corrupted dreamscapes, Blood-iron fortresses, gravity-warped chasms, or bioluminescent fungal jungles.
- Monsters adapt to their environment, growing more alien and resilient on deeper floors.
- Valuable Essences, Mana Stones, and relics tempt explorers to risk deeper ventures.

### Tools of Survival
- **Wayfinders:** Arcane navigators essential for mapping, sensing floor shifts, and returning safely.
- **Safe Zones:** Rare sanctuaries provide brief respite from dangers.

### Mechanics
- **Exploration & Mapping:** Floors shift subtly over time; repeated exploration may reveal shortcuts, hidden portals, or hazards. Accurate mapping increases survival odds.
- **Resources:** Food, water, and breathable air are finite. Certain floors may alter hunger, fatigue, or mana consumption rates.
- **Environmental Hazards:** Gravity anomalies, shifting terrain, magical storms, or temporal distortions can cause injury, disorientation, or delayed effects.
- **Portal Stability:** Not all portals are stable. Some may malfunction, transporting explorers to unintended floors or locations. Activation may require Mana, ritual components, or successful Survival checks.
- **Isolation & Risk:** Being cut off from known portals increases danger. Long-term entrapment escalates fatigue, hunger, and mental stress, impacting Attributes and Skills.
- **Loot & Economy:** Mana Stones, Essences, and relics recovered in Hollowdeep hold monetary, magical, or mechanical value in Solasfall, incentivizing risk-taking.
- **Party Dynamics:** Coordinated parties have advantages in combat, resource management, and exploration; solo explorers face increased danger and limited options.
