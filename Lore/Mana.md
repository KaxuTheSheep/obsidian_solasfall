A fundamental particle of creation — the raw substance from **Magic** which draw their power.

### Nature
Mana is neither physical nor spiritual, but a **liminal energy**, existing between the material and metaphysical. It flows through all living things, the land, and the air, though only trained individuals or divine beings can harness it effectively.

### Function
- Acts as a **chemical and magical catalyst**, powering spells, enchantments, and divine miracles.
- When concentrated, it forms **Mana Stones**, stable crystalline vessels capable of storing and releasing mana in measured amounts.
- All major magical infrastructure — including the Veil and the Rift to the Hollowdeep — depends on refined mana or Mana Stones for operation.

### Sources
- Naturally generated through the **World’s Breath**, an invisible cycle that renews ambient mana over centuries.
- Found in abundance within the Hollowdeep, where unstable realms and collapsed realities compress raw mana into mineral form.
- Artificial extraction is possible but inefficient and dangerous, often resulting in **Mana Overload Syndrome** for unprotected casters.

### Manifestation
Mana has no visible form, texture, or sound. To the untrained, it is **nothing at all** — yet to mages, it is unmistakable. Its presence is _felt_, not seen:
- As a **pressure in the air**, like a storm about to break.
- A **tug beneath the skin**, as though the body remembers something ancient.
- A **vibration of thought**, faint but alive, threading through the fabric of the world.
