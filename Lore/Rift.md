### Overview
A Rift is a magical gateway linking Solasfall to the Hollowdeep. Though it appears as a simple portal, its existence relies on complex arcane engineering by Royal Mages, drawing subtle energy from the Fallows to sustain a stable connection. The Rift is the only sanctioned means for explorers to enter the Hollowdeep, acting as both a lifeline and a gate to untold dangers.

### Access & Mechanics
- **Rift Cycle:** Every 4 weeks, The Royal Mages weaken the Veil to open a Rift for exactly 1 hour in solasfall. During this window, all entrants begin at The Shattered Expanse.
- **Entry Positioning:** Explorers entering earlier start near the central hub of The Shattered Expanse, while later arrivals are positioned closer to portals leading to deeper floors. The further from the centre, the more perilous the environment becomes.
- **Temporal Shift:** Once the Rift closes, Hollowdeep time decouples — 7 Hollowdeep-days pass while only 1 day passes in Solasfall.
- **Return Window:** After 7 Hollowdeep-days, a portal reopens for 1 hour. Those who fail to return are stranded for 28 Hollowdeep-weeks, facing near-certain death unless extraordinarily resourceful.

### Structure
- The Rift directly connects Solasfall to The Shattered Expanse.
- Stable portals lead to subsequent floors, some requiring Mana, rituals, or arcane mechanisms to activate.
- Although portals are permanent, access beyond Floor One is often restricted or dangerous without proper preparation.

### Usage & Influence
- **Explorers Guild:** Primary sanctioned users, relying on the Rift to manage expeditions, harvest Mana Stones, and retrieve valuable Essences.
- **Rebel Group:** Utilize the Rift and Hollowdeep to conceal operations and hide from authorities.
- **Cultists:** Collaborate with the Rebels to access forbidden zones or gather obscure resources.
- **Unofficial Use:** Some illicit parties attempt to exploit the Rift, often resulting in severe consequences due to its dangers.

### Hazards & Considerations
- The farther an explorer ventures from the central hub, the more hostile the environment becomes.
- During the Rift opening, the temporary weakening of the Veil allows brief visual bleeds of the Fallows beyond — bleeding hues and corrupted landscapes visible for the duration. This is a known side effect of the weakening process and carries no direct mechanical consequence.
- Navigational errors, lack of preparation, or straying from mapped paths can be fatal.

### Tools & Navigation
- **Wayfinders:** Arcane navigators are essential for mapping the Rift’s spatial anomalies, sensing floor shifts, and ensuring safe returns.
- Explorers without proper guidance risk becoming disoriented, trapped, or hunted by monsters inhabiting the Hollowdeep.
